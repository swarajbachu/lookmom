Mental model

Think of a spreadsheet that enforces types, uniqueness, and relationships — and that many clients can read/write safely at once.

SQL names this a relation. The engine doesn’t care about display order unless you ORDER BY. What matters is the schema:

  • Columns declare type & nullability
  • Constraints protect integrity
  • Queries declare what, not how

The planner chooses how to fetch rows (scan, index, join order). That’s why indexes matter so much later.
