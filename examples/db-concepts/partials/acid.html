ACID makes multi-step changes safe

A transaction groups statements so they succeed together or not at all. ACID is the classic guarantee set.

transactions
A

Atomicity

All statements commit, or none do. No half-applied transfers.

C

Consistency

Constraints hold before and after — FKs, checks, unique keys.

I

Isolation

Concurrent transactions don’t see each other’s dirty mid-states.

D

Durability

Once committed, data survives crashes (WAL / fsync / replicas).

Why apps care

“Debit Alice, credit Bob” is two writes. Without a transaction, a crash between them loses money. With BEGIN … COMMIT, the database either applies both or rolls back to the prior balance.
